Principal DPS

The Principal leads the school with clear vision and strong values, focusing on academic excellence, pastoral care and community engagement. They guide curriculum plans, mentor teachers and ensure every student has the support to succeed.

Headmaster DPS

The Headmaster maintains high teaching standards and student discipline. They coordinate daily school operations, support teachers, and help implement programs that improve learning outcomes and wellbeing for students.

Deputy Headmaster DPS

The Deputy Headmaster assists with school management, student welfare, and administrative duties. They act as a bridge between staff and leadership and help maintain a safe, orderly learning environment.

DPS (Divisional Public School) is committed to nurturing young minds through balanced academics, sports and moral education. Our staff and leadership work together to create a caring and ambitious environment for every student.
